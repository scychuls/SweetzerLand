      About Us

      Welcome to Sweetzerland!
      As students from Cavite State University, Silang,
      we noticed many of our beloved childhood treats becoming harder to find.
      Driven by nostalgia and a desire to preserve these sweet memories,
      we created this platform to be a dedicated hub for classic Filipino candies,
      allowing everyone to rediscover the simple joys and flavors that sweetened our youth.
